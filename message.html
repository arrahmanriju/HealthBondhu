Messages

Message from the

Managing Director

We know healthcare services are very expensive and time consuming. Lack of accurate information and proper treatment is unimaginable. Who does not want to reduce the cost of treatment, save money and time? Everyone wants savings. We are befriending those in Bangladesh who are working tirelessly in the health sector to provide proper services to the patients and increase their prosperity. We are trying to come up with a platform called Health Bondhu, as well as through the platform we build friendships with patients so that they can easily learn about the exact information, cost, quality and location of treatment. It will be easier to reduce the cost of treatment by comparing the cost according to the service provider. And those who are providing services will also spread all over the country.

Nafizul Islam

Managing Director
Health Bondhu Limited

Message from the

Chief Executive Officer

Health Bondhu is working to make healthcare easier for patients, establishing a link between health and technology. Due to the covid-19 pandemic, health tech has become clear to everyone. This is a positive aspect for us. Health Bondhu will play a key role in bringing HealthCare startups and healthcare providers together under one umbrella for achieving sustainable development. We are moving forward with the goal of representing Bangladesh in the international arena with the aim of achieving SDG-3 (Health and Welfare) by digitizing healthcare in Bangladesh.

Md. Rajikul Hasan

Chief Executive Officer
Health Bondhu Limited
